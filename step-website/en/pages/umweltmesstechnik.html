Ion Mobility Spectrometer (IMS)

The IMS is a mobile gas-analysis system for air- and process control as well as environmental analysis.

Fields of Application

  • Detection of narcotics and drugs
  • Detection of toxic and dangerous substances
  • Environmental protection, especially emission control and monitoring
  • Analysis in food industry
  • Air control for workplace safety
  • Medical diagnostics

Features

  • High sensitivity and selectivity
  • Fast response
  • Continuous monitoring (measurement signal every 2-30 sec.)
  • Low power consumption
  • Minimal effort for service and maintenance
  • Compact and rugged design
  • Portable as well as stationary devices available
  • Serial interface for a external laptop or PC
  • Programmable measuring sequences
  • Analysis software

Available IMS-Models

  • IMS-GSM: Gas trace monitor
  • IMS-NARC: Narcotic agents monitor
  • GC-GSM: Extended model GSM, fitted with a GC-column
  • Bio-IMS: Biological IMS

Photo Ionisation Detector (PID)

Detector for hydrocarbons in the ppb range.

Fields of Application

  • Safety at work: MAK value monitoring, benzene, arsine, phosphine, toluene, xylene
  • Environmental protection, especially emission monitoring
  • Clean room monitoring
  • Monitoring/ tracking in case of accidents, monitoring of danger areas
  • Monitoring of laboratory flues and glove boxes
  • Monitoring of interior room air

Features

  • High sensitivity, minimised cross sensitivity
  • Fast response time
  • Low power consumption
  • Minimal effort for service and maintenance
  • Compact and rugged design
  • Portable as well as stationary devices available
  • Serial computer interface
  • Programmable measurement sequences
